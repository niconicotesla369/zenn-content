## 1. The Curse of "Conversational AI"

As long as you are "chatting" with ChatGPT or Claude, your workload will never decrease.
Every time the AI generates output, you have to verify it, correct it, and think of the next prompt. This is **"Management Overhead"**.

This is the true nature of "AI intensification." The smarter the AI gets, the more output it produces, and the human becomes the bottleneck.

## 2. The Solution: Autonomy, Not Chat

The solution proposed by OpenClaw is simple: **Stop chatting.**

- **Delegate Execution Authority**: Give the AI permissions to manage files, execute code, and deploy. Ask for reports, not drafts.
- **Agent Mesh**: Let multiple specialized agents review and fix each other's work.
- **Immortal Infrastructure**: Build an environment (Cloud Chamber) where the AI keeps solving problems while you sleep.
